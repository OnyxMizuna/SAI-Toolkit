S.AI Toolkit

A privacy-first browser extension that enhances the SpicyChat experience — improved layout, generation statistics, a rich message editor, message recovery, and optional cross-device sync through your own Google Drive.

What it does

  • Sidebar & layout enhancements — a resizable sidebar plus classic and compact layouts.
  • Generation statistics — see the model, token counts, and sampling settings behind each message.
  • WYSIWYG message editor — format messages without wrestling with raw markup.
  • Message Recovery — never lose a long message to a failed send (opt-in).
  • Google Drive Sync — back up and sync your stats and settings across devices using your own Google Drive (opt-in).

Privacy first

Everything runs locally in your browser by default. No analytics, no tracking, and no data sent to the developer. The only time data leaves your device is when you explicitly enable Drive Sync — and even then it goes to your Google Drive using the narrow drive.file scope, never to us.
